About Us

Welcome to Le Royal Hotel, a top-rated 4-star hotel in Marina 5, Zone 24. We're perfectly located near the New Alamein City, offering easy access to all services for a truly luxurious and convenient stay. Enjoy our beautiful outdoor view, free private parking, a lovely garden, plus our own bar and restaurant. We provide free Wi-Fi in all areas. Many rooms have a private terrace, and every apartment comes with a kitchen equipped with a fridge, microwave, and kettle. Our 24-hour front desk staff speak Arabic and English, always ready to help you. At Le Royal Hotel, we're here to ensure your comfort, elegance, and outstanding service.
